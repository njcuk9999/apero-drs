Updated header keys

- HIERARCH DRS RES OBJN            // objname to be used by the DRS || DRSOBJN, BC_OBJNM
- HIERARCH DRS RES GAIAID          // Gaia id to be used by the DRS || BC_GAIAID
- HIERARCH DRS RES RA              // The ra [in deg] used by the DRS || BC_RA
- HIERARCH DRS RES DEC             // The dec [in deg] used by the DRS || BC_DEC
- HIERARCH DRS RES PMRA            // The pmra [mas/yr] used by the DRS  || BC_PMRA
- HIERARCH DRS RES PMDE            // The pmde [mas/yr] used by the DRS || BC_PMDE
- HIERARCH DRS RES PLX             // The parallax [mas] used by the DRS || BC_PLX
- HIERARCH DRS RES RV              // The RV [km/s] used by the DRS || BC_RV
- HIERARCH DRS RES GMAG            // The Gaia G mag used by the DRS || BC_GMAG
- HIERARCH DRS RES BPMAG           // The Gaia BP mag used by the DRS || BC_BPMAG
- HIERARCH DRS RES RPMAG           // The Gaia RP mag used by the DRS || BC_RPMAG
