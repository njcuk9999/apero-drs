APERO Documentation¶

Latest version: 0.8.001¶

APERO is a pipeline designed to reduce astrophysical observations (specifically from echelle spectrographs). It is the official pipeline for:

  • SPIROU (SPectropolarimeter InfraROUge) on the Canada-France-Hawaii Telescope CFHT.

APERO Publications:

  • APERO: A PipelinE to Reduce Observations – Demonstration with SPIRou

APERO can also be used for:

  • NIRPS HE (Currently under construction)

  • NIRPS HA (Currently under construction)
